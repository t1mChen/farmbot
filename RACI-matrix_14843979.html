HarvestX : RACI matrix

Created by Xinxin Guo, last modified on Sep 28, 2023

RACI matrix, (AKA. Responsibility Assignment Matrix), is a project organisational tool used to clarify the roles and responsibilities of individuals within a project process, which prevents confusion and improve team collaboration effectively.

  1. Responsible (R): This is the person who is responsible for implementing the work or task. They are the individuals who are directly responsible for completing a specific activity or deliverable. (There is at least one role of R assigned for each task)

  2. Accountable (A): The "A" designates the person who is ultimately accountable for the task completion. This individual ensures that the work gets done and is responsible for the overall outcome.

  3. Consulted (C): These are the individuals who are responsible for providing feedback and guidance for a specific task.

  4. Informed (I): Individuals who should be aware of all up-to-date information of the task.
