Ethic report：

We split the ethic report into three components:

  1. Lack of Code Transparency

  2. Conflictive Personal Interests

  3. Data Privacy Concerns

For each component, we decide to talk about the Possible Reasons for the concern, its impact to the project and strategies to solve it.

Addressing these ethical issues is crucial for the success of the project. It's important to continually monitor and adapt strategies as needed to maintain ethical standards and ensure project success. Additionally, documenting ethical guidelines and processes can help guide future team members and provide a reference for handling similar issues in the future.
