Over this sprint, our team has successfully developed several new features as mention before in the sprint planning phase as well as the requirement documents (e.g. user stories). During this majorly development focused sprint, our team maintains frequent communication either via hosting team meeting or discussing ideas via group chat. Furthermore, we did not lose any progress in documenting our development process, that is to adjust and add standards and plans to ensure that our solutions are delivered as expected.

 Retrospective

Miro (Team member’s opinion)

Things we did well -

  • Clear and effective team communication: this enables us to allocate tasks towards each of our team member with a fair distribution, also, ensuring that everyone is on the same page with any updates or changes happening.

  • Task estimation strategies. We have estimated the length of each task based on the difficulty ratings, and therefore, in combination with our communication strategies, maximising productivity.

  • Creative thinking & User focused thinking: Since our main focus of this project is to follow the requirements by the client, we have generated creative ideas to maximise the attractiveness of the demo interface by applying the measures of practicability of user purchasing a Farmbot device.

Things that can be improved -

  • Docking of contents developed by the team: Sometimes, the time spent on developing a specific feature may take longer or shorter time than estimated.

  • Hidden difficulties / problems: There could be some unexpected problem happens suddenly during the development of the project. For example, the crash of Virtual machine in an unexpected circumstance setting may impact the team member’s progress greatly.

Steps for improvement -

  • To solve the problem of content integration and progress maintaining, we can work more flexible based on each team member’s own person circumstances or availability.

  • Instead of solving unexpected problems on someone’s own, the team member may propose their ideas and possible solutions to the team, so roles may be reallocated instantly based on the new changed situations.
