Planning:

We planed to complete all must have tasks in sprint 2, 7 tasks in total.

In the first two weeks of the sprint, each team member will complete one task, for a total of five tasks. After completing each task, it will undergo a code review and unit test (Role allocation refers toSprint 2 Planning )by another team member to ensure accuracy. Once confirmed to be error-free, it will be merged into the develop branch( Inspected by Jiahao Shi ).

In the third week, we will finish the rest 2 tasks, after that we will conduct tests for the overall changes and perform user experience test ( Sugon Christjaroon ).
